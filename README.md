# Privacy-Policy-for-Kynooe
Privacy Policy for Kynooe
Kynooe is used to control and configure modular robotic arm devices.
The app may access Bluetooth, local network, location, camera, and video-related data to support device discovery, connection, configuration, control, and user-initiated local training workflows.
When users enable training features, video data may be transmitted to and stored on a server designated and managed by the user, such as a local server on the user’s own network.
Kynooe does not provide developer-operated cloud storage or remote training services for this data.
Data is used only for app functionality and user-initiated training workflows.
The app does not sell personal data.
